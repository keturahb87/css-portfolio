Neck, back and shoulders

The goal of this of massage is to release muscle tension, increase circulation, and leave you feeling relaxed and rejuvenated, helping to reduce both physical discomfort and mental stress.

Face, neck, and hands

This massage is a gentle, soothing treatment that targets the muscles, skin, and pressure points of the face, neck, and hands. It not only promotes relaxation but also stimulates blood flow, helping to rejuvenate the skin and relieve tension.

All feet

A foot massage is a deeply relaxing experience that focuses on relieving tension in the feet, which can be especially soothing after a long day of standing or walking.

Aroma Therapy

Orange

Brazilian orange oil has a sweet, citrusy scent that is known for its mood-boosting and calming properties. It can help reduce feelings of stress, anxiety, and depression.

Peppermint

Peppermint oil is a popular remedy for tension headaches and migraines. It has a cooling effect that soothes the muscles and promotes relaxation.

Eucalyptus

The menthol-like scent of eucalyptus is effective for soothing sinus congestion. It helps to reduce inflammation in the nasal passages and makes it easier to breathe.
